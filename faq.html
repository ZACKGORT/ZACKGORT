Zack Gort FAQ — Reason first, resolution always.

Scroll moderately to experience the animations. Strategy that ships. Systems people actually use.

What do youactually do?

I turn messy product ecosystems into clear, usable experiences. Discovery → IA/taxonomy → prototyping → design systems → tight handoff. Less theater, more throughput.

Strategistor Designer?

Both. I facilitate like a strategist (problems, goals, constraints) and ship like a designer (flows, components, content, specs). Strategy that never hits the UI is just a memo.

Where do youadd the most value?

Alignment. I get marketing, product, and engineering solving the same problem, in the same language, on the same timeline. I document decisions so velocity isn’t personality-dependent.

Approachto Research?

Do enough to be dangerous early; keep learning as we ship. Competitive audits, content/IA reviews, lightweight qual with stakeholders/users, and synthesis that feeds the roadmap.

DesignSystem Philosophy?

Practical first. Systems should be canonical, implementation-friendly, and living—tokens, usage guidance, and examples wired to actual code (not a static PDF).

ShortTimelines?

Name constraints early, pick the critical path, and build just enough scaffolding to ship safely. Componentize, reduce novelty, and prefer decisions that won’t age out in a quarter.

ToolsandStacks?

Whatever keeps the team fast and honest. Figma for design + docs, Notion/Confluence for decision logs, and a component stack that matches the codebase (e.g., React + Tailwind). Headless (Contentful et al.) when structure matters.

What dohandoffslook like?

Receipts: flows, variants, empty-state logic, content guidelines, accessibility notes, and acceptance criteria. I include “why” notes so future-you doesn’t have to reverse-engineer intent.

Communication Style?

Plain language. Problem → evidence → options → recommendation. I write it down, show tradeoffs, and keep the artifact current so it remains a source of truth.

Background?

Design lead turned product-minded IC/manager. Agency, platform, and in-house across commerce, B2B, and enterprise. Formed by constraints, sharpened by shipping.

How dowe work together?

Give me a problem owner, an engineer, and access to the people who feel the pain. I’ll map the system, propose a plan, and start delivering visible, testable increments immediately.

Availability  & Engagements?

Senior IC/lead, fractional product/design, or short, surgical engagements to unblock discovery, IA, or systemization. Flexible on timezone and cadence.
